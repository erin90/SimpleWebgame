About

Survive the Shake is a quick simulation of a student (the player) experiencing an earthquake. It is based on real earthquake drill policies and tips. The outcomes of the player's choices will inform the which actions to avoid and actions to do apply when this natural disaster occurs. The goal of this game is to equip students the proper way of handling disasters such as earthquakes. This is a friendly 1 player game that provides scnarios that can happen when an earthquake occurs.
